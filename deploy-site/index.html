Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Is Resume For You completely free?
Yes, building and editing your resume is completely free — no sign-up, no credit card. You can generate, preview, and customise your resume at no cost. PDF download is available from ₹9 (India) or $0.99 (international).
Do I need to create an account?
No account needed. Open the website, fill in your details, and your resume is ready in minutes. Your data stays in your browser and is never stored on our servers.
How does the AI resume builder work?
You enter your experience, education, and skills. Our AI generates professional, ATS-optimised bullet points and a summary tailored to your job title. You can edit everything before downloading.
What is ATS optimisation?
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) software is used by most companies to filter resumes before a human reads them. Our builder scores your resume against ATS criteria and suggests improvements so your resume passes automated screening.
Can I build a portfolio website?
Yes. Click 'Build Your Portfolio Website' to generate a free professional personal site. Customise the template, add your photo, and download the HTML to host on GitHub Pages, Netlify, or any hosting service — or share a link instantly.
Can I upload my existing resume?
Yes. Upload a PDF or DOCX resume and our AI extracts your details automatically. You can then edit, improve, and re-download in a new template.
How many templates are available?
35+ professionally designed, ATS-friendly resume templates — from clean and minimal to creative and tech-focused. All are optimised for PDF output and recruiter readability.
Is my data private?
Your resume data is stored locally in your browser only. Nothing is uploaded to or stored on our servers. Your personal information stays private.
How do I write a resume with no experience?
Include your education, internships, volunteer work, college projects, freelance work, and relevant coursework. Our AI can generate professional bullet points even from minimal input — just describe what you did, the tools you used, and any outcomes. A strong skills section and a well-written summary can compensate for limited work history.
What is the best resume format for freshers in India?
For fresh graduates in India, a one-page reverse-chronological resume works best. Start with a short professional summary, followed by education, internship experience, projects, and skills. Recruiters at Indian companies scan resumes quickly — keep it clean, ATS-friendly, and one page.
How do I write a professional summary for my resume?
A professional summary should be 2–3 sentences highlighting your job title, years of experience, top skills, and the value you bring. Our AI generates a tailored summary based on your details — you can also edit it directly on the resume after generating. Avoid generic phrases like "hard worker" — focus on specific skills and achievements.
Can I download my resume as a Word document?
Yes. After generating your resume, click "Download DOC" to get an editable Microsoft Word file. This lets you make manual edits in Word or upload it directly to job portals that require a .docx file. PDF download is also available.
How do I make my resume pass ATS screening?
To pass ATS screening: use standard section headings like "Work Experience", "Education", and "Skills"; include keywords from the job description; avoid tables, columns, and graphics inside the resume body; and use a clean, single-column layout. Resume For You's built-in ATS score checker analyses your resume and gives specific improvement suggestions.
What is the difference between a resume and a CV?
A resume is a concise 1–2 page document tailored for a specific job, focusing on relevant experience and skills. A CV (curriculum vitae) is a comprehensive multi-page document covering your full academic and professional history — used mainly in academia and research. In India, "CV" and "resume" are often used interchangeably to mean the same 1-page document.
How long should a resume be?
For most job seekers — especially freshers and those with under 10 years of experience — a resume should be exactly 1 page. Senior professionals with 10+ years may use 2 pages, but only if every line adds value. Recruiters typically spend 6–10 seconds on a first scan, so brevity matters more than length.
Can I save my resume and come back to edit it later?
Yes. Your resume data is automatically saved in your browser's local storage, so it persists when you close and reopen the tab — no account needed. You can also share a link to your resume that encodes all your data, allowing you to reopen and edit it on any device.
Can I save my resume online without a physical copy?
Yes. Sign in with Google to save your resume securely to the cloud — no physical resume required. Access and edit your resume from any device, anytime. Your data is always available when you need it, without needing to carry or store a physical copy.
Can I use this resume builder for jobs in India?
Yes. Resume For You is built with Indian job seekers in mind. PDF download is priced at ₹9 (vs $0.99 internationally). The templates and AI content are suitable for Indian companies, MNCs, startups, IT firms, and government job applications. It works well for Naukri, LinkedIn, and direct company portals.
What should I put in the skills section?
Include technical skills (programming languages, frameworks, tools, software), domain knowledge, and certifications relevant to the job. Limit to 10–15 of your most relevant skills — avoid listing overly basic items like "Microsoft Word" unless specifically required. Resume For You will reference your listed skills in AI-generated bullet points.
Can I create different resumes for different jobs?
Yes. You can regenerate your resume multiple times with different job titles, skill emphasis, or descriptions to optimise content for different roles. Each generation produces fresh, AI-written bullet points tailored specifically to the job title you specify.
Is the AI-generated content original?
Yes. The AI generates original content based on your specific experience, job title, company, and skills — it is not copied from templates or other resumes. Each generation produces unique bullet points and summaries tailored to your profile.
