Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI resume builder work?

Simply enter your job title, company names, skills, and experience details. The AI — powered by Claude (Anthropic) — instantly generates professional bullet points, a compelling summary, and tailored content for each role. You review, edit if needed, and download your polished resume as a PDF.

Is the AI-generated resume content original?

Yes. Every bullet point and summary is generated fresh based on your specific job title, skills, and experience details. The AI does not reuse generic templates — it crafts unique content tailored to you every time.

Can AI write my entire resume?

The AI writes the hardest parts — professional bullet points, your career summary, and skill descriptions. You still provide the factual details such as dates, company names, job titles, and your educational background. This keeps your resume accurate and authentic while saving hours of writing time.

Which AI model powers the resume builder?

Resume For You uses Claude by Anthropic for AI content generation. Claude is one of the most capable large language models available and excels at producing clear, professional, human-sounding writing — making it ideal for resume content.
