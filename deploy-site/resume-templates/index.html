Resume Templates — FAQs

How many resume templates are available?

Resume For You offers 35+ professionally designed resume templates spanning six categories: Modern and Professional, Creative and Bold, Minimal and Clean, Dark and Striking, Coloured and Vibrant, and Classic Corporate. New templates are added regularly and all are free to use with no account required.

Are all resume templates ATS-friendly?

Yes. Every single template — including the creative, dark, and coloured options — has been designed to pass ATS scanning. We use clean HTML structure, standard section headings, and avoid tables, text boxes, and images that confuse ATS parsers. You can choose any design you love without worrying about compatibility.

Can I switch templates without re-entering my data?

Yes. Your resume data is stored separately from the template design. Switch between any of the 35+ templates with a single click and see your content instantly re-formatted in the new style. Experiment freely until you find the look that best suits your industry and personality.

Which resume template is best for freshers?

For freshers and new graduates, the Minimal and Clean category works best. These templates make limited work experience look structured and professional, with extra emphasis on skills, education, and projects. The Modern and Professional templates are also a strong choice for corporate and finance graduate roles.
